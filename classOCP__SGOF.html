默认构造函数

0th column: The gas saturation (Sg) 1th column: The corresponding gas relative permeability (Krg) 2th column: The corresponding oil relative permeability when oil, gas and connate water are present. (Krog) 3th column: The correspondin oil-gas capillary pressure (Pcgo = Pg - Po) (bars (METRIC), psi (FIELD)) Values should be level or increasing down the column.
